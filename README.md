Comprehensive Car Market Analysis: Fleet Acquisition Strategy
📌 Overview
This project provides a data-driven approach to optimizing fleet acquisition for a logistics enterprise. By analyzing a comprehensive dataset of vehicle specifications, pricing, and efficiency metrics, this analysis identifies high-value vehicle categories that balance performance (Horsepower) with operational sustainability (Fuel Efficiency).

🚀 Key Objectives
Budget Optimization: Identify and exclude luxury pricing outliers.

Operational Efficiency: Determine the "sweet spot" for vehicle body types that offer reliable performance with low fuel consumption.

Strategic Recommendations: Provide actionable insights for procurement teams to reduce long-term fuel overhead.

🛠️ Technical Stack
Data Processing: Python (Pandas, NumPy)

Querying: DuckDB (SQL-based data manipulation)

Visualization: Seaborn, Matplotlib, and Tableau Public

Environment: Kaggle Notebooks

📈 Project Highlights
Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA): Performed comprehensive data cleaning to handle missing values and inconsistent naming conventions.

SQL-Driven Insights: Used SQL queries to benchmark MSRP pricing and segment inventory by drivetrain profile.

Visual Narrative: Developed a series of comparative visualizations (Scatter plots & Bar charts) to communicate complex trade-offs between horsepower and MPG to non-technical stakeholders.

💡 Executive Recommendations
Institute a Capital Ceiling: Limit per-unit acquisition costs to avoid market outliers and luxury pricing premiums.

Standardize Fleet Procurement: Focus procurement on Sedan and Hatchback profiles to capture high fuel efficiency relative to power.

Prioritize Sustainable Drivetrains: Increase the ratio of non-gasoline vehicles to align with long-term cost reduction and corporate sustainability goals.
